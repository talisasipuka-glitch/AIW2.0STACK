OFFER PACK, personal-injury-lawyers

## Core positioning sentence

"I help personal injury law firms win the trust of accident victims who are
hurt, stressed, and unsure which firm to call right after a crash, so they
choose this firm over the dozens of similarly-worded competitors they find
in the same search."

## One-line public offer (IG bio / agency homepage)

"I build high-converting websites for personal injury law firms."

## Stage of business

Stage: **No portfolio at all.**

Reasoning: Talisa's reputation/portfolio assets are warm relationships (a
former real estate broker, one law firm contact), not finished website
work. Design self-score is 3/5. Per the framework's stage table, this maps
to the "no portfolio" offer shape.

Offer shape: "I'll design your homepage upfront, for free. If you love it,
you pay. If not, you walk."

## Offer mechanics

- **Risk reversal**: Free homepage design upfront, built on the
  personal-injury-lawyers factory template. The firm sees a working,
  on-brand homepage before paying anything.
- **Price anchor**: Three tiers, the firm buys the middle one.
  - Tier 1, Homepage only: $1,500 to $2,500
  - Tier 2 (anchor), Full site with case-type landing pages, FAQ, blog
    starter, and the trust stack (settlement results bar, press band,
    founder story, testimonials): $4,000 to $6,000
  - Tier 3, Full site plus monthly content/SEO retainer: $6,000 build +
    $500 to $800/month
- **Payment terms**: Free homepage upfront. On approval, 50% deposit to
  build out the full site, balance on delivery. No payment plan needed at
  this stage since the upfront work is free, simplicity over financing.

## End-customer language to echo (from 02-customer-voice.md)

- "Kept me updated" / "always there and always concerned for my case"
- "Explained every aspect of the process"
- "Treat you as family" / "caring people"
- "Fight for you to get the best [outcome] back"
- "Your road to justice starts with a single call"
- "We offer free initial consultations to help you understand your rights
  and your options"
- "Legal issues don't wait for a 'good time' to happen"

## Top 3 trust signals to lead with (from 05-trust-signals.md)

1. "No Win, No Fee" / contingency guarantee, stated near every CTA, not
   just once.
2. Settlement-dollar proof tagged by case type (a number the visitor can
   match to their own situation).
3. Communication/responsiveness proof with a named-person guarantee, the
   direct answer to the #1 fear ("being ignored or left in the dark").

## Cold DM, long (180 to 240 words, with strategy preview)

```
Hey [First Name],

I've been researching personal injury firms in [City] and spent some time
on [Firm Name]'s site. A couple of things stood out that I think are
costing you calls.

First, there's no settlement-dollar figure anywhere on the homepage. The
firms converting best in this space (Morgan & Morgan, Dolman Law) put a
number like "$5M recovered for a car accident client" right where a
visitor first lands, because someone who was just in a crash wants to know
fast whether you handle cases like theirs and whether it's worth the call.

Second, "No Win, No Fee" isn't repeated near your contact form. The reviews
I read for firms in this space all say the same things, "they kept me
updated," "they explained every step," "they treated me like family." That
tells me the #1 fear after an accident isn't cost, it's being ignored. Your
site should say, in plain language, who picks up the phone and how fast.

I built a homepage concept for [Firm Name] using that structure: a
settlement-results bar tagged by case type, a press/awards strip, a named
attorney with a personal story, and a 4-field "Free Case Evaluation" form
that doesn't scare people off with a 7-question intake.

I'll build it for free. If you love it, you pay. If not, you walk, and if
I've wasted your time, I'll send you a $25 Starbucks gift card for the
trouble.

Worth a 15-minute screen-share to show you what I built?

[Your name]
```
