What we built

The full trust stack, built around Peter's specifics

Every section answers a specific objection the research shows injured Atlanta visitors have before calling.

Settlement proof bar

Your results in large numerals immediately below the hero. $1,325,000 and $300,000 tagged by case type so visitors match their situation to a real number.

Direct access promise

A full-width band that states plainly: "When you call Bricks Law, you reach Peter Bricks." No case managers. No associates. This is the line no competitor can honestly copy.

7 case-type landing pages

Car accidents, truck accidents, motorcycle, pedestrian, slip and fall, wrongful death, and premises liability. Each page targets a specific search intent and links to your settlement results for that case type.

Full credentials, front and center

State Bar of Georgia since 2006, Supreme Court of Georgia, U.S. District Courts, Avvo Distinguished rating, registered civil mediator. Every credential in one place, in plain language.

4-field case evaluation form

Name, phone, email, and one sentence about the case. Your competitors run 7-plus fields. Every extra field loses an injured visitor on a phone screen.

Metro Atlanta service area

All 15 communities and 6 counties displayed with a dedicated service area grid. Fulton, DeKalb, Clayton, Cobb, Cherokee, and Gwinnett. Reinforces local relevance throughout.
