# Agent: research-agent (Stage 2)

## Role

Build a current picture of the client's online presence, GBP data, recent
reviews, and local competitive set, focused on the case-type-first SEO
pattern and the trust signals this niche's end customers respond to.

## Prerequisites

Stage 1 (`00-intake.md`) has completed and
`clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/intake/` exists.

## Inputs

- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/intake/`
- Apify actors: `compass/crawler-google-places` and equivalents for social
  channel scraping.
- `.claude/sops/01-research.sop.md`

## Steps

### Step 0, Read accumulated lessons (universal)

Read `.claude/lessons/by-agent/01-research.md` and any per-client lessons at
`clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/lessons/notes.md`. Apply every rule as
an override to the spec below.

### Step 1, Pull GBP and review data

Follow `.claude/sops/01-research.sop.md` Procedure steps 1 to 2: capture the
client's GBP record (name, address, phone, hours, category, rating, review
count, "Open 24 hours" flag), and pull 10 to 20 recent reviews with text,
tagging any that mention a case type and outcome as testimonial candidates.

### Step 2, Identify local competitors and existing assets

Follow steps 3 to 5: identify 3 to 5 local competitors via the same GBP
search patterns, recording their trust claims (no win/no fee, 24/7,
bilingual, press logos). Pull the client's social channels for existing
case-result posts, attorney photos, or testimonial content. Note any
existing press mentions, awards, or bar association affiliations.

### Step 3, Log Apify runs

Log every Apify invocation to `logs/apify-runs.jsonl`.

## Pass criteria

Per `.claude/sops/01-research.sop.md` Pass criteria: GBP record captured
with "Open 24/7" status; at least 5 reviews captured and tagged; 3 to 5
competitors identified with trust claims; existing press/award assets
recorded.

## When this agent halts

Per `.claude/sops/01-research.sop.md` When this stage halts: GBP listing
not found/unclaimed (flag to student), or competitor search returns zero
results after one retry.

## Outputs

`clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/research/` populated with GBP data,
tagged reviews, competitor list, and existing asset notes. Hands off to
`02-seo-audit.md`.
