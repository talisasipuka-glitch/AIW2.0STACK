# Agent: strategy-agent (Stage 5)

## Role

Confirm the client's sitemap, with the 7 case-type pages as the primary
structural decision, and decide content composition for StatsBar,
PressBand, WhyChooseUs, and office locations.

## Prerequisites

Stages 1 to 4 (`00-intake.md`, `01-research.md`, `02-seo-audit.md`,
`03-asset-scraper.md`) have completed.

## Inputs

- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/research/`
- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/seo/`
- `clients/[Client Name]/[Client Name] Assets/`
- `research/02-niche-research/personal-injury-lawyers/09-sitemap.json`
- `.claude/sops/04-strategy.sop.md`

## Steps

### Step 0, Read accumulated lessons (universal)

Read `.claude/lessons/by-agent/04-strategy.md` and any per-client lessons at
`clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/lessons/notes.md`. Apply every rule as
an override to the spec below.

### Step 1, Confirm the sitemap and case-type pages

Follow `.claude/sops/04-strategy.sop.md` Procedure steps 1 to 2: confirm the
sitemap against `09-sitemap.json`. For each of the 7 case types, decide
covered (with SEO title/H1, settlement figure status, FAQ set) or omitted
(do not ship empty placeholder pages).

### Step 2, Decide section composition

Follow steps 3 to 7: confirm the homepage section order is unchanged
(Header, Hero, ProcessSteps, StatsBar, PressBand, WhyChooseUs,
PracticeAreasGrid, FounderStory, Testimonials, FAQSection, CTABand, Footer).
Decide StatsBar composition (4+ figures target, fallback framing if fewer),
PressBand content source (real press logos vs. local badges),
`location_pages[]` status, and the WhyChooseUs bullet set reflecting only
verified claims.

### Step 3, Write strategy output

Write the confirmed sitemap and strategy decisions to
`clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/strategy/`.

## Pass criteria

Per `.claude/sops/04-strategy.sop.md` Pass criteria: sitemap confirmed with
per-case-type status; StatsBar composition decided; PressBand source
decided; `location_pages[]` confirmed; WhyChooseUs bullets reflect only
verified claims (no unconfirmed "24/7" or "bilingual").

## When this agent halts

Per `.claude/sops/04-strategy.sop.md` When this stage halts: fewer than 2
case types covered (escalate for direction decision), or zero disclosable
settlement figures and no aggregate figure (escalate, StatsBar needs
quantified proof).

## Outputs

`clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/strategy/` populated with confirmed
sitemap and section composition decisions. Hands off to `05-copy-deck.md`.
