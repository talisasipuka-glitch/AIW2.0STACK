# Agent: brand-dna-agent (Stage 7)

## Role

Extract the client's brand DNA into the canonical 32-key shape contract,
populating the niche-specific fields: license number, attorney bios,
per-case-type settlement figures, and Stage 6's locked copy.

## Prerequisites

Stages 1 to 6 have completed, including `05-copy-deck.md`.

## Inputs

- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/intake/`
- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/research/`
- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/copy/`
- `clients/[Client Name]/[Client Name] Assets/`
- `references/brand-dna.shape.js`
- `design-synthesis/SKILL.md`
- `.claude/sops/brand-dna-agent.sop.md`

## Steps

### Step 0, Read accumulated lessons (universal)

Read `.claude/lessons/by-agent/brand-dna-agent.md` and any per-client
lessons at `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/lessons/notes.md`. Apply
every rule as an override to the spec below.

### Step 1, Populate company, contact, and team fields

Follow `.claude/sops/brand-dna-agent.sop.md` Procedure steps 1 to 3:
populate `company.name`, `company.shortName`, `company.licenseNumber`
(hard requirement), `contact.*`, `address.*`, `hours.*` (only mark 24/7 if
confirmed), and `team.founder`/`team_members[]` with headshots from Stage 4.

### Step 2, Populate services, case-type pages, badges, and copy

Follow steps 4 to 8: populate `services[]` from Stage 5's confirmed case
types; populate `pages.services.items[]` with `settlementFigure` only where
disclosable; populate `press_logos[]`/`trust_badges[]`; populate
`why_choose_us[]`, `process_steps[]`, `reviews.*`, `faq[]`, all `copy.*`
from Stage 6 verbatim; populate `location_pages[]` per Stage 5.

### Step 3, Compute confidence and gate

Follow steps 9 to 10: compute extraction confidence, with
`company.licenseNumber`, `team.founder`, and at least one fully-populated
`pages.services.items[]` entry as required fields. If confidence < 0.70,
halt at the approval gate (`/approve-brand-dna`).

## Pass criteria

Per `.claude/sops/brand-dna-agent.sop.md` Pass criteria:
`brand-dna.json` validates against the shape contract;
`company.licenseNumber` non-empty; `team.founder` populated with headshot;
every covered case type has a matching `pages.services.items[]` entry;
`settlementFigure` only where confirmed disclosable; all `copy.*` carries
Stage 6's locked copy without paraphrase.

## When this agent halts

Per `.claude/sops/brand-dna-agent.sop.md` When this stage halts: confidence
< 0.70 (approval gate); `company.licenseNumber` missing (hard halt
regardless of confidence); no `team.founder` entry (hard halt).

## Outputs

`clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/brand/brand-dna.json`. Hands off to
`07-5-brand-resonance.md` (if applicable) and `08-hero-image.md`.
