# 5 - Strategy

Implements: Stage 5.

## Purpose

Translate the research, SEO audit, and harvested assets into the client's
sitemap and content strategy, with the 7 case-type landing pages as the
primary structural decision.

## Inputs (per-client)

```
clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/research/
clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/seo/
clients/[Client Name]/[Client Name] Assets/
```

## Procedure

1. Confirm the sitemap against `09-sitemap.json`: Home, About, Practice
   Areas, the 7 case-type pages (`/case-types/{slug}`), Testimonials, FAQ,
   Contact, Blog. For each case type the client does NOT cover (per Stage 1
   intake), decide: omit the page from the build, or relabel it to a case
   type the client does cover. Do not ship an empty or generic placeholder
   page for an uncovered case type.
2. For each case type that IS covered, confirm: the SEO title/H1 from Stage
   3, the settlement figure (if disclosable), and the case-type-specific
   FAQ questions identified in Stage 3.
3. Decide the homepage section order per `09-template-spec.md` Section 4:
   Header, Hero, ProcessSteps, StatsBar, PressBand, WhyChooseUs,
   PracticeAreasGrid, FounderStory, Testimonials, FAQSection, CTABand,
   Footer. This order is fixed by the template; strategy confirms WHICH
   content fills each section, not whether to reorder them.
4. Decide the StatsBar composition: which case types get a dollar figure
   (Dolman-density target is 4+ figures per `09-template-spec.md` Section
   4, item 4). If the client has fewer than 4 disclosable figures, decide
   the fallback framing (aggregate "millions recovered" figure, or a mix of
   case-type figures and non-dollar proof points).
5. Decide the PressBand content: real press logos if the client has them
   (Stage 4), or fall back to local bar association / "Best of [City]"
   badges per `09-template-spec.md` Section 4, item 5.
6. Confirm `location_pages[]` status: empty array for single-office clients
   (Office Locations section omitted), or populated for multi-office
   clients per Stage 1.
7. Confirm the WhyChooseUs bullet set: no fee unless we win, available
   24/7 (only if true), direct attorney access, "not a settlement mill"
   positioning, bilingual service (if applicable), per
   `09-template-spec.md` Section 4, item 6 and `05-trust-signals.md`.
8. Write the strategy document and the confirmed sitemap to
   `Pipeline Data/strategy/`.

## Pass criteria

- Sitemap confirmed: all non-omitted case-type pages have a title, H1,
  settlement figure status, and FAQ set.
- StatsBar composition decided with at least the available disclosable
  figures, plus a fallback framing if fewer than 4.
- PressBand content source decided (real press logos vs. local badges).
- `location_pages[]` status confirmed.
- WhyChooseUs bullet set confirmed, reflecting only verified claims (no
  "24/7" or "bilingual" unless Stage 1 confirmed it).

## When this stage halts

- Fewer than 2 case types are covered by the client. This materially
  changes the template's primary SEO strategy; escalate to the student for
  a direction decision before continuing.
- The client has zero disclosable settlement figures AND no aggregate
  recovery figure. Escalate: the StatsBar and PracticeAreasGrid sections
  need at least some quantified proof, per `05-trust-signals.md` signal #2.

## Niche-specific notes

For this niche, the following pattern applies (derived from
`research/02-niche-research/personal-injury-lawyers/09-template-spec.md` +
the niche playbook):

The 7 case-type landing pages are the template's primary SEO
differentiator (`06-seo-landscape.md`): "[case type] lawyer [city]"
outranks generic "personal injury lawyer [city]" for case-specific
searches, and most small firms do not ship these pages. Strategy's central
job for this niche is making sure every covered case type gets a fully
realized page carrying the FULL trust stack (per `09-template-spec.md`
Section 5), not a stub. If the client covers all 7, ship all 7. If the
client covers fewer, ship exactly those, do not pad the sitemap with
case types the firm does not practice.
