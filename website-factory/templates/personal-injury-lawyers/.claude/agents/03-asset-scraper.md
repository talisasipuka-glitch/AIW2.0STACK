# Agent: asset-scraper-agent (Stage 4)

## Role

Harvest the client's logo, attorney headshots, office photos, case-result
graphics, and trust badges. Halt the pipeline if no logo is found. Reject
gavel/scales-of-justice stock imagery as a primary asset.

## Prerequisites

Stage 1 (`00-intake.md`) and Stage 2 (`01-research.md`) have completed.

## Inputs

- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/research/`
- `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/intake/`
- `asset-scraping/SKILL.md`
- `.claude/sops/03-asset-scraper.sop.md`

## Steps

### Step 0, Read accumulated lessons (universal)

Read `.claude/lessons/by-agent/03-asset-scraper.md` and any per-client
lessons at `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/lessons/notes.md`. Apply
every rule as an override to the spec below.

### Step 1, Scrape the client's site, GBP, and social channels

Follow `.claude/sops/03-asset-scraper.sop.md` Procedure steps 1 to 3: scrape
for logo, attorney headshots (one per named attorney from Stage 1), office
photos, case-result graphics, and trust/affiliation badges (state bar,
membership forums, press logos, BBB/local awards).

### Step 2, Filter and sort assets

Follow steps 4 to 6: explicitly reject gavel/scales-of-justice/courthouse
stock imagery as a primary asset (flag for Stage 5/9 instead). Sort
harvested images into logo, attorney headshots (tagged by name), office
photos, case-result graphics, trust badges/press logos, and testimonial
photos. Cross-check the case-type list from Stage 1 for figure/graphic gaps.

## Pass criteria

Per `.claude/sops/03-asset-scraper.sop.md` Pass criteria: logo found and
saved (hard requirement); at least one attorney headshot per named attorney
or a flagged gap with a plan; office photos collected if available; trust
badges/press logos collected and tagged; no gavel/scales-of-justice imagery
passed through as primary.

## When this agent halts

Per `.claude/sops/03-asset-scraper.sop.md` When this stage halts: no logo
found anywhere (hard halt, escalate to student to obtain/commission a
logo); no attorney headshot exists for any named attorney with no photo
shoot scheduled (strong flag, since this is the niche's strongest trust
element).

## Outputs

`clients/[Client Name]/[Client Name] Assets/` populated with sorted,
tagged assets and gap flags. Hands off to `04-strategy.md`.
