| CTABand | 0.85 | 0.15 | Consistent final CTA, same as Home. |

### Page-to-body-sections mapping

| Page | Body sections | brand-dna paths |
|---|---|---|
| About | FounderStory (expanded), WhyChooseUs, Testimonials | `team.founder`, `team_members[]`, `copy.founder.*`, `why_choose_us[]`, `reviews.items[]` |
| Practice Areas | PracticeAreasGrid (all 7 cards) | `services[]`, `pages.services.items[]` |
| Testimonials | Testimonials (full list, grid not carousel) | `reviews.items[]`, `reviews.rating`, `reviews.totalReviewCount` |
| FAQ | FAQSection (full list) | `faq[]` |
| Contact | ContactDetails (address, phone, hours, map) + LeadForm variant="page" | `address.*`, `contact.*`, `hours.*`, `copy.formHeader` |
| Blog | BlogGrid (cards: cover, title, excerpt, date, category) | `blog_posts[]`, `blog_categories[]`, `copy.blog.*` |

Each body section in the mapping table above is scored against the
corresponding Home page region threshold (e.g. the About page's FounderStory
instance is checked against the Home page FounderStory threshold of 0.85).

## OVERALL WEIGHTED-MEAN TARGET

Aggregate target: **0.90** weighted mean across all regions, with no region
below its threshold.

Per-page actuals from the niche wireframe:
- Home: ~0.895 (within band)
- Case-Type Landing Page: ~0.884 (within band)
- Generic Interior Page shell and Blog Post: inherit thresholds from Home
  and the per-page tables above; no standalone weighted mean computed.

Blended overall target across the build: **0.89**. This is the number the
design-fidelity-qa-agent should compare the build's overall weighted SSIM
against when reporting a single site-wide figure. Home and Case-Type both sit
within the 0.90 band individually; the blended 0.89 reflects the Case-Type
page's slightly lower weighted mean carried across 7 of the site's 10 routes.
